## 2. Production Volume Tiers

### 🥉 The "Hobbyist" Tier (~$18/mo)
- **Volume**: 1-2 videos per month.
- **Tools**: Free Leonardo.ai tier, Runway Standard (625 credits), ElevenLabs Starter.
- **Best For**: Starting out and learning the workflow.

### 🥈 The "Pro" Tier (~$62/mo)
- **Volume**: 4-8 videos per month (Weekly Uploads).
- **Tools**: Leonardo.ai Basic, Runway Pro (2,250 credits), ElevenLabs Creator.
- **Best For**: Serious creators looking to scale and hit the YouTube algorithm consistently.

### 🥇 The "Full Studio" Tier (~$110+/mo)
- **Volume**: 15+ videos per month (Daily Uploads).
- **Tools**: Runway Unlimited ($76), ElevenLabs Higher Tier.
- **Best For**: Full-time automated channels with daily content.

### ✨ The "Zero-Cost" Strategy ($0/mo)
If you want to spend **$0**, use this daily credit rotation:
- **Video**: **Kling AI** (66 daily credits) or **1min.AI** (15k daily credits).
- **Voice**: **Google AI Studio (Gemini TTS)** or **Luvvoice** (No limits).
- **Images**: **Leonardo.ai** (150 daily tokens) or **Playground AI**.
- **Volume**: ~2-4 high-quality shorts or 1 long-form video per week.

---

## 3. How to Save Money in 2026
- **Yearly Billing**: Most of these tools offer **20-25% discounts** if you pay for the whole year upfront.
- **API Credits**: Use the Pay-As-You-Go model for Leonardo.ai to only pay for the exact number of frames you generate.
- **Ma Concept**: Because Ghibli uses "Ma" (quiet, still moments), you can use shorter 5-second animations and loop them, saving significant Runway credits.

> [!NOTE]
> These estimates are for the **Automation Pipeline**. If you continue using the manual dashboard, your costs may stay on the "Low" side for longer as you can carefully pick each generation.
