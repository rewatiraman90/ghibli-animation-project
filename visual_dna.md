# Visual DNA: Studio Ghibli Aesthetic (2026)

This document establishes the "Style Anchor" to ensure visual consistency across all generated clips.

## Style Anchor: Watercolor Ghibli
- **Texture**: Analog hand-painted watercolor textures, visible soft brushstrokes, non-digital paper grain.
- **Color Palette**: Pastel meadows, lush moss greens, soft cerulean skies, warm sunset ambers.
- **Lighting**: Volumetric sunbeams (Tyndall effect), soft golden hour glow, gentle overcast lighting with no harsh shadows.
- **Character Design**: Simple expressive facial features, large curious eyes, rounded organic shapes.

## Identity Anchor: Elara
Every prompt for the main character must include this description:
> **Physical Anchor**: A 12-year-old girl with a soft, round face, expressive chestnut eyes, and messy shoulder-length black hair held back by a simple red ribbon. She wears a faded oversized indigo sweater with cream-colored overalls.

## Master Reference Image Prompt
> `Studio Ghibli style, a 12-year-old girl with a soft round face and messy black hair with a red ribbon, wearing an indigo sweater and cream overalls, standing in a lush green meadow with soft watercolor textures and volumetric sunbeams, hand-painted aesthetic, 90s cel-shaded anime look --ar 16:9 --v 6.1 --stylize 250`

## Animation Guardrails
- **Movement**: Subtle wind in hair, swaying grass, slow blinks.
- **Camera**: Mostly static wide shots or slow pans/zooms to emphasize "Ma".
